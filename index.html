🏛️ About the Hackathon

DJS NSDC

DJS NSDC is a student-run chapter focused on data science and AI, housed under the Artificial Intelligence and Data Science Department at Dwarkadas J. Sanghvi College of Engineering, Mumbai. This initiative operates in association with the Northeast Big Data Innovation Hub.

🔥

Monstadt Hackathon

Monstadt 2.0 is a hackathon where innovative minds come together to address real-world challenges.

⚡

Team Size and Rules

Team must consist of 3-4 Members. Multi-domain participants are allowed in a single team.INTER-COLLEGE teams are ALLOWED.

Domains & Prizes

🌐

Web/App Development

Focused on building interactive and user-friendly websites and applications using modern frameworks and tools. Encourages students to enhance problem-solving, UI/UX design, and full-stack development skills.

🤖

AI/ML Domain

Dedicated to exploring artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data-driven solutions. Provides hands-on exposure to algorithms, predictive models, and real-world applications of AI.

❤️

Social Cause Domain

Bridges technology with social impact by creating projects that address community needs. Aims to use innovation for welfare, awareness, and solving real-life societal problems.

🔗

Blockchain Domain

Centers on decentralized technologies like blockchain, cryptocurrencies, and smart contracts. Encourages learning about secure, transparent, and futuristic applications in finance, supply chain, and beyond.
